What. The. FAQ?

Why should I use Butter?
To run engaging, structured, and joyful sessions! It puts all your facilitation tools in one place, ending the nightmare of juggling tabs.
What types of sessions is Butter best for?
Workshops, training sessions, design sprints, all-hands meetings, and any collaborative sync where engagement matters.
Is Butter free?
Yes! You can get started for free on our basic plan. We also have Pro and Enterprise plans for advanced features.
How many participants can join a session?
Up to 200 participants can join depending on your plan tier. Perfect for large all-hands or interactive webinars.
Is Butter GDPR compliant?
Absolutely. We take privacy and security very seriously. All European data is hosted in the EU.
